Welcome to pod-ette, the podcast companion to the literary magazine -ette! In our inaugural episode we talk with -ette founders Beth Hahn and Nora Maynard. Our featured piece is "If I Could Remake the World" by Andrew Bertaina and we’ll hear from Andrew about the inspiration for the piece. Original music for pod-ette is by Chester Jankowski.
